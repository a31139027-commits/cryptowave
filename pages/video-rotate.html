About This Tool

This tool uses FFmpeg.wasm to rotate and flip video files inside your browser. You can rotate 90° clockwise, 90° counter-clockwise, or 180°, and optionally combine that with a horizontal or vertical flip. The output is always exported as MP4 (H.264).

About This Tool

This tool uses the Web Speech API built into modern browsers to synthesize speech from text. All processing is local — nothing is sent to any server. The available voices depend on your operating system; Chrome typically offers the most via Google's TTS engine. Voice settings are saved between sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text sent to a server?
No. This tool uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API for speech synthesis. Your text is processed locally by your operating system's speech engine and is never transmitted to any external server.
What languages are supported?
Available languages and voices depend on the voices installed on your operating system and browser. Most modern systems include English and several other languages. You can install additional voices through your OS settings.
Can I download the audio?
Yes. After generating the speech, you can download the audio as a WAV file to save and use locally or share with others.

About This Tool

This tool uses FFmpeg.wasm to crop video inside your browser using the crop filter. Choose a preset aspect ratio (16:9, 4:3, 1:1 square, 9:16 portrait) for center-cropping, or manually enter X/Y offset and width/height for a precise pixel crop. Output is exported as MP4 (H.264).

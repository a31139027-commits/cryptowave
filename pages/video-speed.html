About This Tool

This tool uses FFmpeg.wasm to change video speed inside your browser. It applies the setpts filter to video frames and the atempo filter to audio, keeping video and audio perfectly in sync at any speed. The output is exported as MP4 (H.264).

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my videos uploaded to a server?
No. All speed adjustment is done using FFmpeg.wasm which runs entirely in your browser. Your video files never leave your device and are never transmitted to any server.
Does the audio speed change too?
Yes. Both video and audio are adjusted together to stay in sync. When you change the playback speed, the audio pitch is also adjusted to prevent chipmunk or slowed-down voice effects.
What speed range is supported?
You can adjust video speed from 0.25x (quarter speed slow motion) to 4x (four times speed). Values outside this range may cause audio/video sync issues or encoding errors.

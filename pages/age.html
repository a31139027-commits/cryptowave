About This Calculator

This calculator determines your exact age using your browser's date engine. It correctly handles leap years, month-length variations, and daylight saving time. You can also calculate how old you were or will be on any specific past or future date using the "As of" field.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is age calculated?
Age is calculated from your birth date to today's date, or to a target date you specify. The result reflects the exact completed years, accounting for whether your birthday has occurred in the current year.
Is time zone factored into the age calculation?
The calculation is date-only and does not factor in time zones. Both the birth date and target date are treated as calendar dates, giving a consistent result regardless of your location.
Can I see my age in months and days?
Yes. In addition to years, the calculator shows the precise breakdown of your age in years, months, and remaining days, giving you an exact measure of how old you are to the day.

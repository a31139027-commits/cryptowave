About This Tool

All counting happens instantly in your browser as you type. Reading time is estimated at 238 words per minute (average adult reading speed). Speaking time uses 130 words per minute. Your text is never sent anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text uploaded to a server?
No. All word counting and analysis runs entirely in your browser. Your text is never transmitted to any server and remains completely private on your device.
What statistics are counted?
The tool counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs. Additional metrics such as estimated reading time may also be shown.
Is there a text length limit?
No. There is no imposed limit on text length. You can paste documents of any size, limited only by your browser's available memory.
